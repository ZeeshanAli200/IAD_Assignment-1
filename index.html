Introduction


PAK ITALIAN MARBLE was formed in 1998, is among the Leading Processor & Exporter of PAKISTAN oriented Marble as well as Imported Granite and Marble in Pakistan Market.

With the passage of time, we have enhanced our production capacity, adopting new techniques & Markets. Also keen in development and operating Quarries moderating with unique and advance heavy duty machines. We give our valuable customers a peace of mind in all terms; Quality Satisfaction, in Time Delivery, New Attractive Colors, Competitively Low Price, Creative Art Work and Customer Satisfaction.

Why Choose Us?



PAK ITALIAN MARBLE is a direct manufacturer & supplier of natural marble sandstone, limestone etc., established in 1995. We operate advance machinery including wiresaw, dozers, excavators and other machines in our quarries. Gang saws, block cutters, monolama machines for block cutting, polishing lines, other cutting & splitting machines in our processing units to produce largest quantities of blocks, slabs & tiles for international and domestic market. We have been consistent in providing our customers excellent products & services at competitive prices which is a complete team work of efficient and committed personnel.
