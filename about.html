About Us

Exporter of PAKISTAN oriented Marble as well as Imported Granite and Marble in Pakistan Market. With the passage of time, we have enhanced our production capacity, adopting new techniques & Markets. Also keen in development and operating Quarries moderating with unique and advance heavy duty machines. We give our valuable customers a peace of mind in all terms; Quality Satisfaction, in Time Delivery, New Attractive Colors, Competitively Low Price, Creative Art Work and Customer Satisfaction. PAK ITALIAN is the reliable source to its customers around the World and we make them feel it happily in a lasting business relationship. PAK ITALIAN offer in natural Marble, Onyx & Granite various products such as:

  • Quarry Blocks Raw and Squared
  • Slabs 2cm and 3cm, Random and cut-to-size Polished / Unpolished
  • Tiles Standard Size 60x60x2cm, 30x60x2cm, 40x40x1cm, etc Polished, Honed, Antique etc
  • Exterior Decorative Stone; Bush Hammered, Rough Surface Chissling,
  • Mosaics, Molding, Decorative Products

Why Choose Us?

PAK ITALIAN is a direct manufacturer & supplier of natural marble sandstone, limestone etc., established in 1995, it is the largest marble brand of Pakistan, It has 2 big marble factories over 75000sqm in Pakistan and own 5 quarries for marble, limestone and sandstone. Our showroom and export sales center is located in Karachi, Lahore and Islamabad. It is over 1500sqm and have many different kinds of marbles and stones choice display. PAK ITALIAN export to over 100countries in the world, include United States, Canada, Middle east, Italy, Norway, Swden, Australia,South Africa, etc.
